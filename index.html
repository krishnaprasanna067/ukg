What is UKG Pro WFM?

UKG Pro WFM (formerly known as Kronos Workforce Dimensions) is a cutting-edge workforce management solution that enables organizations to efficiently manage employee time, attendance, scheduling, compliance, and labor analytics. Designed to optimize workforce productivity, it automates HR processes, reduces manual effort, and ensures seamless integration with payroll and HCM systems. Businesses across industries rely on UKG Pro WFM to enhance efficiency, improve employee engagement, and drive operational excellence.
